ScARtch


In summer 2017, one of our founders took a university Compilers and Programming Languages class for which a final project activity was required.
As he had already witnessed the immersive power of VR, he chose to develop a VR educational visual programming environment inspired by MIT's Scratch. The result was formidable: a very intuitive programming language (that is especially suitable to teach the fundamentals of coding to children) was paired with a natural interaction paradigm to make programming as easy as snapping puzzle pieces together.

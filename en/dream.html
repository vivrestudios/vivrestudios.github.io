Dream UniVRse


We are actively working on our first game to be released on Steam!

Being avid gamers ourselves, we always dreamed of designing our own game. When we first tried on a VR Headset, we immediately realised we had the possibility of doing it in a revolutionary way, bringing the player directly inside our vision.
Dream UniVRse is a collection of minigames that put you in everchanging situations, constantly challenging your ability to understand where you are and what you must do in order to advance. Venture through tens of different settings and find out new ways to interact with Virtual Reality.
